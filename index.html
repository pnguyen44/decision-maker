Welcome To The Decision Maker

This app allows you to create lists with the option to have the app automate the selection process. It will help you make decisions quickly and easily. The app will select from the remaining items on the list that has not been checked off as completed by you.

Create list of items

Create a list and add item to include in your decision making process

Eliminate options from the decision making

You can uncheck items to remove them from the decision making

Randomly select an option for you

Click 'decide' and it will randomly select from your list of options
